About Ingenuity

Our Mission

Ingenuity was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to make advanced AI technology accessible to everyone, regardless of technical background or financial resources.

Despite the high costs associated with AI compute infrastructure, we are committed to keeping this platform permanently accessible and free for generating tokens and running inference.

Our Commitments

We believe that access to AI technology should be democratized, allowing everyone to benefit from these powerful tools regardless of their financial situation. Our approach prioritizes fairness, transparency, privacy, and human-centered design in everything we do.

Beyond providing AI services, we're dedicated to education. Our comprehensive resources are designed to help users understand the technology they're using.
